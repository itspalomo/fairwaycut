Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

The short version

  • The FairwayCut iOS app collects no personal information.
  • All imported video, audio, and pose-overlay rendering runs on your device. Nothing is uploaded to us, and we operate no servers that receive your content.
  • We use no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party services that receive data from the app.
  • The app requests Photo Library access only to import videos you choose. If you export or share a replay clip, that happens only when you explicitly choose a destination in iOS.

1. Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy describes how the FairwayCut iOS application ("the App") handles information. The App is published by itsPalomo ("we," "us," "our"). It does not apply to the open-source FairwayCut command-line tool, third-party websites, or any other software you may install.

2. Information we do not collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store on our servers, sell, or share any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, postal address, or other contact information
  • Account credentials (the App has no account system)
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA) — we do not request App Tracking Transparency permission because we do not track you
  • Crash reports or diagnostic logs sent to us
  • Usage analytics, screen views, taps, sessions, or behavioral data
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings sent to us or uploaded to our servers
  • Health, fitness, or biometric information
  • Contacts, calendars, reminders, or files outside the App

3. Information processed locally on your device

The App processes the following information solely on your device. None of it is transmitted to us or to any third party:

  • Video and audio you import from Photos or Files for post-session swing analysis. Audio tracks within imported videos are analyzed on-device to detect swing impact.
  • Pose-estimation results derived from your video using on-device machine-learning models (BlazePose / MediaPipe) when you choose to generate a pose overlay. The model runs entirely on your device.
  • Local app data such as replay window settings, processed session history, swing timestamps, and imported source labels, stored locally using iOS standard storage.

This information stays in the App's sandboxed storage on your device unless you choose to export or share a generated clip through the iOS share sheet. If you delete the App, locally stored app data is removed by iOS.

4. iOS permissions the App requests

iOS will prompt you the first time the App needs each of the following. You may grant or deny each individually, and revoke them at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security or Settings > FairwayCut.

  • Photo Library — to import existing videos you select from Photos.

The App can also import videos you explicitly select from the iOS Files picker. That access is brokered by iOS for the file you choose. If you deny Photo Library permission, the App will continue to work where possible; some import features may be unavailable.

5. Third parties

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs that receive data from your use of the App. We have no analytics, advertising, attribution, A/B testing, error reporting, or social-login providers. We have no servers that handle your content, and we do not receive copies of any video, audio, or analysis data.

If you choose to export or share a replay clip, iOS presents the system share sheet and the destination you choose may receive a copy. Any handling of that shared copy is governed by the privacy policy and terms of the destination you select, not by us.

Apple processes information related to App Store distribution, in-app crash reports you choose to share with developers, and standard device telemetry. That processing is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not this one.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because we collect no information from anyone, we do not have data about children to delete. If you believe a child has provided personal information through any FairwayCut channel, contact us and we will respond promptly.

7. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA / CPRA, and similar laws)

Privacy laws in your jurisdiction may give you rights such as the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, or to opt out of sale or sharing. Because we do not collect, sell, share, or otherwise process your personal information, we have no information about you to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or opt out of selling. We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as those terms are defined under California law.

To remove information held locally on your device by the App, delete the App from your device.

8. Security

By default, nothing leaves your device unless you choose to export or share a replay clip. The security of your swing recordings and locally stored session data depends on the security of your device and your iOS configuration (passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, automatic backup settings). If you enable iCloud Photos, or share a clip to another app or service, that copy may be backed up, synced, or processed under the policies of Apple or the destination you selected, not by us.

9. International users

The App can be used worldwide. Because no information is transmitted to us, no international transfer of your personal data occurs as a result of using the App.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date above and post the updated policy at this URL. Material changes will be reflected in updated App Store metadata. Your continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy: itspalomo.dev@gmail.com.
