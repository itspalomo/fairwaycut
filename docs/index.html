About FairwayCut

FairwayCut is a post-session golf swing import, replay, and analysis tool. The iOS app processes imported video and audio entirely on your device, and can generate optional pose-overlay clips on-device.

These legal pages apply to the iOS app only. The open-source FairwayCut command-line tool is available at github.com/itspalomo/fairwaycut and is distributed separately under the repository's MIT License, which provides the software "AS IS" without warranty.
